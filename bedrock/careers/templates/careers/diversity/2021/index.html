Diversity & Inclusion at Mozilla: 2021 Results

Executive Summary

As an organization with employees, volunteers, and community members spanning the globe we strive to ensure that our people, products, and partners reflect the communities we serve and that all contributors feel empowered to join our movement. Investing in diversity and inclusion is imperative to our mission of ensuring the future of the internet and the future of our society. #AllWelcomeHere

Each year we aim to account for our progress and challenges in advancing our commitment to being a diverse, equitable, inclusive, and anti-racist organization. This 2022 disclosure captures our progress through 2021. This year, we are pleased to provide data that captures the intersectional experiences of our employees in recognition of the complexity of our identities. In assessing our data and programs we have found that we have made strides in some areas and have growth opportunities in others. From our engagement survey data we have gleaned that our programming and initiatives have made a difference in the experience of our employees. We made strides in some areas of representation and remained stagnant in other areas.
